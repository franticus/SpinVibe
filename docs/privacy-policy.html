Privacy Policy — SpinVibe

At SpinVibe, your privacy is as important to us as the excitement you feel when the roulette wheel spins. We believe that protecting your personal information is part of building trust with every player. This Privacy Policy explains in detail what data we collect, why we collect it, how we safeguard it, and what choices you have regarding your personal information. By being transparent and clear, we aim to ensure that you always feel safe and respected while enjoying our platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you visit SpinVibe or play our roulette experience, we may collect certain types of information to ensure smooth operation and a better experience. This may include details you provide directly, such as your email address when contacting us, as well as technical information gathered automatically. These details help us tailor the game to different devices and ensure compatibility. Our systems may also track your gameplay activity, including the duration of sessions, the screens you interact with most, and performance-related data. Additionally, we use cookies and local storage to keep the site stable and responsive. These tools allow us to remember preferences and provide a seamless gaming experience every time you return.

  • Your name or email, but only if you choose to contact us directly.
  • Basic device data such as browser type, screen size, and IP address.
  • Interaction data like session length, visited pages, and click behavior.
  • Cookies or local storage to maintain functionality and stability.

2. Why We Collect This Data

The information we collect is used to make SpinVibe more enjoyable, more reliable, and better suited to your needs. Our team analyzes usage patterns to understand which features players love and which ones need improvement. This feedback loop allows us to design updates that enhance your experience. Beyond optimization, we also use your data to solve technical issues faster and provide better support if you ever encounter difficulties. In some cases, certain data is collected to meet legal and compliance requirements, ensuring SpinVibe continues to operate responsibly and transparently.

  • To optimize performance and create a smoother gameplay experience.
  • To address feedback and resolve technical problems efficiently.
  • To identify which features are most popular and improve weaker areas.
  • To remain compliant with data protection laws and platform rules.

3. How We Protect Your Information

Protecting your data is one of our highest priorities. We use trusted technologies and secure storage methods to keep all information safe from unauthorized access. Only selected and authorized team members may access user data, and only when necessary to improve your gaming experience or provide assistance. We continuously review our systems to maintain high standards of security. Should a security incident occur, we are committed to acting quickly, keeping you informed about what happened, and taking steps to prevent similar issues in the future.

4. When We Share Information

SpinVibe does not sell or misuse your information. Sharing is kept to an absolute minimum, and only in situations where it is necessary to keep the platform running effectively. For example, some anonymous usage data may be shared with analytics providers to understand performance across devices and regions. In rare cases, we may share information with hosting or cloud providers to deliver content reliably. If required by law, we will also cooperate with legal authorities. Rest assured, we always share the minimum amount of data necessary for the situation.

  • Analytics providers, to better understand trends and performance.
  • Legal authorities, only when required by official regulations.
  • Cloud and hosting providers, to ensure stable content delivery.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You are always in control of your personal information. You have the right to request details about the data we hold, to ask for corrections if something is inaccurate, or to request deletion if you no longer want us to keep it. Wherever possible, you can also limit or refuse the use of your data, especially for optional features like analytics or marketing. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, just contact us directly — our team will be glad to respond quickly and assist with your request.

To reach out, email us anytime at spinvibe@gmail.com and we’ll make sure your request is handled with care and transparency.

6. Communication and Updates

From time to time, we may send you updates about SpinVibe — but only if you have chosen to receive them. These may include announcements about new features, improvements, or feedback opportunities. Each email will always include a clear option to unsubscribe, so you remain in full control of what you receive. We respect your inbox and only send relevant, occasional messages that might improve your overall experience.

7. Compliance with Laws

SpinVibe is designed to follow major privacy regulations, including the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act. We carefully review our practices to ensure they meet current legal standards. By staying compliant, we provide players with not just entertainment, but also the peace of mind that comes from knowing their information is handled responsibly. Our policies are regularly updated to align with changes in global privacy frameworks, ensuring that we remain accountable and trustworthy.

8. Contacting Us

If you ever have questions, concerns, or simply want more details about how we handle data, our team is always available. You can reach us at spinvibe@gmail.com and expect a helpful response. We encourage all players to contact us freely — whether for privacy concerns, feedback, or general support. Your trust is the foundation of SpinVibe, and we are dedicated to protecting it.
