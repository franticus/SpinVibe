Your Privacy Matters

At ChipChase, we care about your privacy just as much as you care about having fun while playing. This policy explains what information we collect, why we need it, and how we keep it safe.

1. What We Collect

When you play our game or visit our website, we may collect:

  • Your name or email (only if you contact us directly).
  • Basic device info like browser type, screen size, or IP address.
  • Gameplay interactions — like how long you play or which screens you visit.
  • Cookies or local storage to keep things running smoothly.

2. Why We Collect It

We use this data to:

  • Make ChipChase faster, better, and more fun.
  • Help solve technical issues and respond to feedback.
  • Understand what players enjoy most (and improve the rest).
  • Stay compliant with laws and platform requirements.

3. How We Protect Your Info

All data is stored securely using trusted technologies. Only authorized team members can access it, and only when it’s needed to help improve your experience.

In case of any security issue, we’ll act fast and let you know what happened and what we’re doing about it.

4. When We Share (Spoiler: Rarely)

We may share minimal data in these cases:

  • With analytics providers to understand usage patterns.
  • With legal authorities, but only if we're required to.
  • With cloud or hosting services to deliver content properly.

5. Your Choices & Rights

You can always:

  • Ask what data we have about you.
  • Request corrections or deletion.
  • Say “no thanks” to data usage where possible.

Just reach out at chipchase@gmail.com and we’ll help you right away.

6. Emails and Updates

We might occasionally email you if you've asked us to — for example, about new updates or feedback requests. Every message includes an unsubscribe option.

7. Staying Legal

ChipChase follows major privacy regulations like the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act. We regularly review our processes to make sure we stay compliant.

8. Reach Out Anytime

Got questions? Need help with anything privacy-related? Email us at chipchase@gmail.com.

We're always happy to support our players.
